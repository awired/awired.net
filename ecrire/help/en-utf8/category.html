The categories

To create or modify a category, you simply have to go to the Categories section and click on New category or on the link of an existing category, depending on whether you want to create or modify a category.

Fields

Title
The name of the category. On the default model, the title is displayed in menu and also for each entry. This field is required.
URLed title
The URLed title is the "URLish" form of title. Accents and disturbing characters for URL are deleted. The DC Engine can build it except if you determine a specific one. You can click the "Use" link to fill the field according to your category's title. Note that if the category's title is made of non latin characters, you you must specify a title of your choice. Titles made of 4 figures or more are invalid. In order to be valid they must be accompanied by letters. By example, 2005 will be invalid while year2005 will be perfectly valid.
Description
Description of the category, in XHTML format. This optional field allows you to give a detailed description of the category.
